The mapping

Circle of fifths meets the color wheel.

The hue of each pixel determines its pitch class. Both systems divide a circle into 12 equal steps — a natural pairing.

C 0° Red
G 30° Orange
D 60° Gold
A 90° Yellow
E 120° Green
B 150° Spring
F# 180° Cyan
Db 210° Azure
Ab 240° Blue
Eb 270° Violet
Bb 300° Magenta
F 330° Rose

Saturation → Chord Quality

High saturation produces major chords. Lower saturation yields minor, suspended, or seventh chords. The algorithm also applies functional harmony rules based on scale degree.

Brightness → Octave

Dark pixels play in lower octaves (3), bright pixels in higher octaves (5). This maps the image's light/dark contrast into melodic contour.

Alpha → Velocity

Transparent pixels become soft notes or musical rests. Fully opaque pixels play at full velocity. Consecutive rests merge into longer pauses.
