How it works

From pixel to pitch.

01

Upload an image

Drag and drop any image onto the upload area, or click Choose a File. Photos, paintings, abstract art - the richer the colors, the more complex the composition.

02

Shape your sound

Pick a Mode (Combined, Chords, or Melody), a Key, a Scale, a Scan Path for how pixels are read, and the Length of your piece.

03

Color to Music mapping

Hue maps to pitch via the circle of fifths. Saturation shapes chord quality. Brightness sets the octave. Even transparency controls note velocity.

04

Download & play

Hit Generate and download a standard MIDI file. Open it in any DAW such as Ableton or GarageBand. Assign your own instruments and mix.
